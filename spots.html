MooMooCha 14th Street, 2nd Ave Great Good Bubble Tea that isn't too expensive. My favorite size is the Stubby Cup since there is so much tea in it. Granted, that size is $10. The surrounding area is also pretty quiet. The inside is small, but is clean and has a nice aesthetic.
Barnes & Nobles 17th Street, Park Ave Great It has a great selection of books. The building nice and air conditioned as well. Furthermore, the staff are all nice and really helpful. There's a starbucks on the third floor as well, so that's a plus.
Tompkins Square Park East 10th Street Bad Tompkins Square Park is one of the worst parks in NYC. The park itself and its surroundings smell bad. My nose picks up hints of urine and feces everytime I pass by. The park is also not safe at night, as drug users frequent the area.
